What is Steganography?

Steganography is the art and science of hiding secret messages in a way that prevents detection. Unlike encryption, where the message is altered to appear unintelligible, steganography involves embedding the message within a seemingly harmless mediumâ€”such as an image, audio file, or video. This ensures that the message remains concealed, even when intercepted.

In digital steganography, data is hidden in the pixel values of an image or audio file, ensuring that the changes are imperceptible to the human eye or ear. It is a powerful tool for ensuring privacy and security in the digital world.

About StegoHide

StegoHide is a web-based application that allows users to hide confidential messages inside images, ensuring privacy and security. It offers an easy-to-use interface to encode messages into images and decode them back, all while preserving the integrity of the image itself.



Features

Encode Messages

Decode Messages
